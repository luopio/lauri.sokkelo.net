Stand In Bot

remote controlled robotics

Stand-In-Bot was born from the desire to sleep late but not miss class. It was fueled by the dream of being able to follow lectures while eating breakfast and sipping coffee. From this shared vision the team set out to build a rudimentary prototype in 2008.

Influenced by magnificent projects such as tweenbots, the design tries to keep things simple while taking advantage of the great features offered by the Nokia Internet tablet technology.
